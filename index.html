Where we began

Maximus was founded in 1945 by Chef Vito Cannoli. After returning from the war, Michael Cannoli inherited the family restaurant from his father Vito. Vito Cannoli brought the sicilian flavors of Italian cuisine to the great city of New York which heavily influenced Chef Michael. From a young age, Michael showed promise of becoming a great chef. He would battle each dish as would a gladiator in the collesium. Since its conception, Maximus has received 3 forks from Ristoranti d'Italia and 3 Michelin stars.
